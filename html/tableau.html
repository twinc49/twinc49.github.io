Profits in the United States

As you can see the states are colored based on profit. The lighter colors mean less profit and the darker the green the more profitable the state is. California stands out as one of the most proitable states in the country. Let's look at California more closely.

Profit by City of California

This graph shows profit by city inside the state of California. We can see that L.A., San Diego, and San Francisco have the highest profits. This could be due in part to the higher populations found in those areas.



Profits By Category for California

Now looking at the whole state we can see profits based on the product category. As you can see office supplies are by far the most profitable category for the state. All though Technology isn't drastically far behind. Furniture however has significantly lower profits then the other two categories.


Foreasted Sales by Quarter for California

Finally, we look at the forecasted sales for the upcoming year. It appears 2015 will perform similar to 2014 untill we reach the 4th quarter. At that point the forecast predicts a drop off in sales compared to 2014 when sales increased. We should look deeper into why quarter 4 sales went up in 2014 instead of down like in other years. Hopefully we can find the cause and replicate it this coming year.
